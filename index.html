Recent Projects

Feature Identification System

The Feature Identification System(FIS) is the major project that I did with my two other teammates for our last semester. The FIS aims to reduce the possibility of human error by means of integrating both hardware and software. The product is able to detect for missing features in a component such as pins, bolts and more.

ASPIRE Challenge

The ASPIRE Space Rover challenge tasks students to create a space rover that can search for the presence of water and ice on an outer planet. The video showcases my group's space rover idea, which is equipped with an Arduino water sensor and a temperature gauge. We managed to secure 5th place with our video and prototype.

Global Navigation Satellite System Innovation Challenge

The objective of the Global Navigation Satellite Systems(GNSS) Innovation Challenge is to develop a wearable monitoring solution using high precision satellite positioning system to benefit the elderly and persons with special needs.
